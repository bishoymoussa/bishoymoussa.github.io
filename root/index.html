I'm a Machine Learning researcher specializing in Computer Vision and Multi-Object Tracking at Northeastern University. My work focuses on transformer-enhanced tracking algorithms for complex scenarios, combining deep learning with graph-based approaches.

Computer Vision Multi-Object Tracking Deep Learning Medical AI Graph Neural Networks Transformers

Research

My research centers on improving multi-object tracking in complex scenarios, developing algorithms that can handle occlusions, varying lighting conditions, and multiple camera views. This work has applications in healthcare, security, and human-computer interaction.

Research Areas

  • Transformer-enhanced graphical multi-person tracking
  • Multiple toddler tracking in indoor videos
  • Classification of infant sleep-wake states from video data
  • Machine learning models for cancer prediction
  • Motion analysis for exoskeleton control
